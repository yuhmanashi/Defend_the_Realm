How to play:

  1. Enemies spawn from the left portal and move to the right.
  2. Enemies get stronger after every wave
  3. You lose HP when an enemy reaches the right portal.
  4. Click on characters in shop to select them and then click again on the platform to place them.
  5. Characters will attack targets infront of them based on their respective range.

    6. Modes:

  6. Classic - Game ends after a few waves.
  7. Endless - Play until you lose.
