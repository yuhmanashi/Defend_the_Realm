Character Info

  1. Knight:
      Range: Short
      AtkSpeed: Medium
      Damage: 1
      Targets: All enemies in range
      Cost: $50

  2. Archer:
      Range: Long
      AtkSpeed: Fast
      Damage: 1
      Targets: Single closest
      Cost: $50

  3. Wizard:
      Range: Longest
      AtkSpeed: Slow
      Damage: 2
      Targets: All enemies in range
      Cost: $100

How to play:

  1. Enemies spawn from left portal and move right.
  2. HP is lost when an enemy reaches the right portal.
  3. Click on characters on bottom right to select then click again on platform to place.
  4. Characters will attack targets infront of them based on their range.
  5. Classic Mode - win once you survive a certain amount of waves.
  6. Endless Mode - Test your skills, play until you lose.
